INFORMATION

Your guide to using the CGPA Calculator system

1. About This System

The CGPA Calculator is a simple, fast and user-friendly web system designed to help students calculate their semester GPA and overall CGPA.

2. How to Use the Calculator

  • Go to the Calculator page from the sidebar.
  • Enter the course name, credit hours, and grade obtained.
  • Click + Add Course to add more subjects.
  • Press Calculate CGPA to see your results.
  • The system will display total credit hours and your CGPA.

3. CGPA Formula Used

CGPA = Total Grade Points / Total Credit Hours
Grade Points = Credit Hours × Grade Value

4. Notes

  • Your data is not stored on a server — all calculations are done locally in your browser.
  • The grade scale follows common university standards.
  • Always verify your official results with your university’s academic system.
